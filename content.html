Pyramids of Giza



The pyramids of Giza are a historical monument. They are present in the city of Giza, which is present in Egypt. There are three pyramids at Giza. The three pyramids are the Pyramid of Khufu, the Pyramid of Khafre, and the Pyramids of Menkaure. They were built 4500 years ago. The pyramid of Khufu is the highest of the other two pyramids. The Pyramid of Khufu is also called the Great Pyramid. The pyramids were built to preserve the bodies of dead pharaohs. These pyramids were one of the seven wonders of the ancient world.
